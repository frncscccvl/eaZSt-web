Privacy Policy for eaZSt

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

eaZSt ("the App") is developed by FRANCIS CACCAVALE ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use the eaZSt mobile application and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

1. Data Architecture (Cloud-Assisted Hybrid Model)

eaZSt utilizes a secure hybrid architecture to balance on-device privacy with real-time financial tracking.

  • Local Device Storage (The Vault): We prioritize storing authentication tokens locally on your device's physical hardware using encrypted environments (iOS Keychain and Android Keystore) whenever possible.
  • Secure Backend Processing: To provide 30-day income momentum tracking and real-time push notifications, our secure backend servers act as a processing bridge. Our servers securely handle initial OAuth connections, listen for third-party income events (webhooks), and process strict banking integrations.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

A. Third-Party Service Connections & Webhooks

The App allows you to connect to third-party platforms (e.g., Stripe, Shopify, Patreon, Twitch, Kraken) to visualize your earnings.

  • Data Aggregation: Our secure servers fetch specific, limited financial data (such as 30-day revenue averages) strictly to calculate your passive income momentum.
  • Live Event Processing: For certain services, our backend listens for live income events (e.g., a completed Shopify order) solely to trigger a real-time push notification to your device. We do not store your customers' personal identifying information.

B. Banking Services (Plaid)

We use Plaid Inc. ("Plaid") to securely connect your bank accounts.

  • Plaid collects your login credentials directly (we never see your bank username or password).
  • Plaid provides our secure server with an Access Token, which is stored in our encrypted database.
  • We use this token to fetch transaction data (specifically deposits) to calculate your income flow.
  • Information collected by Plaid is subject to the Plaid Privacy Policy.

C. Push Notifications

To alert you when you earn passive income, we securely store a device identifier token (FCM Token) on our backend. This token is used exclusively to route financial alerts to your specific device and contains no personal identity data.

D. Biometric Data (FaceID / TouchID)

The App uses your device's biometric capabilities to secure your financial dashboard. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. The App never has access to your actual face or fingerprint data; we only receive a secure "Unlock" signal.

E. Crash Logs and Stability

We use crash reporting tools (e.g., Google Firebase Crashlytics) to identify bugs. If the App crashes, it sends an anonymous technical report (e.g., device model, OS version). This data is anonymous and does not contain your financial tokens. Google Privacy Policy.

3. Data Retention & Instant Account Deletion

We believe your data belongs to you. You have the right to revoke our access at any time.

  • Individual Disconnects: You can disconnect any individual service within the App. Doing so immediately deletes the local tokens and instructs our backend to sever the connection with that third party.
  • Full Account Deletion: All user accounts utilize Anonymous Login and the App contains a "Delete All Data" feature. After a user unsubscribes, the app instantly severs all active banking connections (Plaid), erases your anonymous user data, deletes your device identifiers, and wipes all webhook routing maps from our servers. Any subsequent data sent by third parties to our servers will be permanently rejected and dropped.

4. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
